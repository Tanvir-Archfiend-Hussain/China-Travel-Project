Classics Part V Mandakini

Hawalaat

Hawalaat is a 1987 Hindi-language Indian feature film directed by Surendra Mohan, starring Shatrughan Sinha, Mithun Chakraborty, Rishi Kapoor, Padmini Kolhapure, Anita Raj, Mandakini and Amrish Puri.

Param Dharam

Param Dharam is a 1987 Hindi-language action film directed by Swaroop Kumar and produced by Dimpy, starring Mithun Chakraborty, Mandakini, Amrish Puri and Sumeet Saigal.

Jeete Hain Shaan Se

Jeete Hain Shaan Se is a 1988 Bollywood action film directed by Kawal Sharma, starring Mithun Chakraborty, Sanjay Dutt and Govinda. It was the third highest grossing Hindi film of that year, although it is probably best remembered for the song "Julie Julie".
